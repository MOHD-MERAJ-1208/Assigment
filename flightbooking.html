    • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    How do I check my flight details?

    To check your flight details, you have to login to your EaseMyTrip and go to the ‘My Booking’ section on web or app. Once you click on this section, the list of your bookings will be shown. You can click on your already booked trip to see your flight details.

    How do I check if my flight is delayed?

    To check if your flight is delayed or not, go to the flight status page of EaseMyTrip. After that, you can choose the airline name from the drop down menu and then enter your flight number and date of travel. It will display the status of your flight and you will come to know if your flight is on time or delayed. Customers having latest version of EaseMyTrip app will also receive a notification about any changes or delay in flights. Customers subscribed for airline notifications also receive messages from the airlines regarding any delay in flights.

    How do I find my flight terminal?

    Flight terminal is mentioned on the confirmation email that you receive after making bookings. You can also find your flight terminal on the ‘My Booking’ section of your EaseMyTrip account. Information about gate number will be available at the airport during check in time. You can also check your gate number on the airport screens displaying information about arrival and departures times of various flights.

    What Information you get from ‘Flight Status’?

    Flight status option at EaseMyTrip will provide you the real time information of your flight such as gates, delays, estimated takeoff/landing time, etc. EaseMyTrip customers can track a flight through real-time GPS tracking of the flights.
